Privacy Policy for KathaPotha AI

Last updated: December 2025

KathaPotha AI (“the App”), created by Darshana Wijebahu, offers AI-powered Sinhala & English storytelling, learning assistance, and text-generation features. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the app.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Entered Text
When you type text into the App (for stories, prompts, questions), we temporarily process it only to generate AI responses. No personal information is stored.

b. Automatic App Analytics
We may use anonymous data such as:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Crash reports
  • App performance information

c. We Do NOT Collect Sensitive Data
The App does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Location / GPS
  • Address
  • Contacts

2. How We Use the Information

  • Generate AI-based text responses
  • Improve app speed and performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. AI text processing may use trusted third-party AI providers, but: No user identity is collected or stored.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data. While no system is 100% secure, we ensure no personal identifying information is stored.

5. Children’s Privacy

KathaPotha AI is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
