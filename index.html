Privacy Policy for KathaPotha AI

Last updated: January 2025

KathaPotha AI ("the App"), developed by Darshana Wijebahu, provides AI-powered story creation, Sinhala/English text generation, and learning features. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Data
The App does not require account creation. You may enter text for story creation or other AI features. This text is processed temporarily for generating results.

b. Automatically Collected Data
We may collect anonymous analytics data such as device type, OS version, crash logs, and performance metrics to improve the App.

c. No Sensitive Data
The App does not collect personal identity data such as name, email, address, contact number, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data only for:

  • Generating AI responses
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Crash diagnostics

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. AI processing may be handled by trusted service providers (such as APIs you request), but no personal data is stored.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data; however, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
